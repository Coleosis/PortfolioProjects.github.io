About Us

We are a fairly new company established in September, 2021. A few of us had all practiced wood and metal working with a background as electrical technicians, and we all played in a band. We decided our band sucked so we should try our hand at making instruments. Turns out we are very good at that! After a couple years of practice we decided to start our own custom instrument company to make peoples dreams come true. Please navigate to our SERVICES page to explain and request your very own custom made instrument!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a service charge?

Our creators main income is service charges, so we charge an automatic 20% gratuity.

What kind of instruments do you make?

We currently create custom acoustic and electric forms of Guitar, Bass, Ukelele, and Mandolin.

What happens if I cancel my order?

Once an order is placed it is final. If you cancel an order once an order has been placed, a cancelation fee of $200 will be charged to the credit card on file. You will also be charged for any materials ordered as well as the cancelation fee.
